It's the Dice Game!

* Player rolls 6 six-sided Dice until they reach 10,000 points
* Player wins when they reach 10,000 points
** Dice rolls are scored as following:
Single 5's - 50 points ea
Single 1's - 100 points ea
3 of a Kind 1's - 1000 points
3 of a Kind 2-5 - Face value * 100 (4,4,4 is 400)
Straight (1,2,3,4,5,6) is worth 1500 points!
